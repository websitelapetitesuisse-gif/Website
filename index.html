Our story

A place
like no other

In the heart of the city, La Petite Suisse was born from a simple idea: create a place where you eat really well, drink with care, and keep coming back.

Our cooking draws from the great tables without conforming to them. Market produce, live fire, house-made sauces — every plate has something to say.

In the dining room and at the bar, our team is here for you, and for making time pass gently.
